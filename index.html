Most Frequently
Asked Questions

My violets are already dying. What to do?

Assess the condition of the soil. Whether a complete change and removal of the remnants of the old is necessary is up to you to decide. But in many cases it is changed completely.

What causes violet leaves to turn white?

The reasons may be either a lack of nutrients in the soil or a lack of lighting in the apartment.

What is the cause of rot on violet leaves?

This means that the violet is affected by a fungal disease.

How do I choose a violet when purchasing?

Plants with soft drooping leaves and brown spots or pests should be avoided.

When is it necessary to transplant violets?

Violets should be replanted at least 2 times a year. The most favorable time for this is the beginning of spring and mid-autumn.

What is the reason for the white coating on the soil in the pot of violets?

This is due to an excess of salts in the soil.
